WHY TRAVEL HERE?

Bogota, the city of everyone

Tourist, cultural, economic and administrative epicenter of Colombia. Its streets are a legacy of tradition, memories and emotions where multiculturalism and diversity converge.

  • Country: Colombia
  • Language: Spanish
  • Currency: Colombian Peso
  • Population: 8 million People

PLACES

My favorite places in Bogota

El Oro Museum

Would you like to know the origin of the legend of El Dorado?

What I like about it?

With a historical past full of myths and legends, the pre-Columbian Indian tribes were expert goldsmiths, so you can learn all about this history at The Gold Museum, and appreciate the pieces that survived the Spanish conquest.

Adress

Carrera 6 # 15 - 82

Paloquemado Market Square

Would you like to taste the most delicious fruits in the world?

What I like about it?

Of the local cuisine be sure to try the dish: Ajiaco. You will find it in the market square of Paloquemado. Here you can also take the gastronomic tour of the best selection of tropical fruits.

Adress

Av. ciudad de Lima # 25 - 04

Kaputt Club

Would you like to celebrate as only Latinos know how?

What I like about it?

The Bogota club Kaputt boasts four dance floors, an arcade room, and an outdoor space characterized by an old vintage car and graffiti-lined walls. Abstract, curved lights float above the dance floor, giving off a mystical aura.

Av. Caracas # 72a - 10
